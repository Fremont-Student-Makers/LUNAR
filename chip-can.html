Challenge 03

Chip Can Challenge

A precision flight challenge built around light payloads, reliable recovery, and repeatable performance.

What It Tests

The challenge rewards efficient flight, stable ascent, and a clean landing while carrying the chip can payload.

Where It Flies

Chip Can flights can happen at Snow Ranch or Brigantino Park depending on recovery footprint and weather.

Scoring

What Counts

Altitude

Higher is better when the payload stays within the rules.

Recovery

Clean recovery and low drift earn the best impressions.

Build Quality

Tidy build discipline and repeatable results matter all season.

It is a small, standardized payload used to compare flight efficiency and recovery behavior across teams.
You can fly it at club launches where the recovery field and site rules are appropriate.
Focus on consistent boost stability, safe separation, and a recovery system that brings the payload down predictably.
