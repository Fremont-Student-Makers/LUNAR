Challenge 01

American Rocketry Challenge

Student teams design, test, and fly a mission rocket with a real competition timeline and mentor feedback.

How it Works

Teams build to a mission objective, usually involving payload, altitude, and recovery constraints. LUNAR provides coaching on simulation, build quality, and launch-day logistics.

Where Teams Fly

ARC teams fly at the Ohlone Newark Campus site in Fremont, which is reserved for student challenge launches and prep flights.

Team Support

What We Provide

Design Reviews

Iterate on airframe, recovery, and ballast choices before flight day.

Build Nights

Workbench time for motors, templates, avionics, and simulated flyoffs.

Launch Support

Mentors help with range safety, prep flow, and mission timing at the site.
