What We Do

Rocketry for Everyone

From your first Estes kit to a 10-foot aerodynamic carbon-fiber monster, LUNAR provides the community, mentorship, and range space to make it fly.

🏆

Competitive Launches

Participate in altitude challenges, drag races, and egg-lofting contests at our monthly launch days.

🎓

Certification Flights

Our experienced mentors guide you through NAR Level 1, 2, and 3 high-power rocketry certifications.

🔬

Workshops & Build Nights

Learn airframe construction, avionics, recovery systems, and motor selection from seasoned club members.

👩‍🚀

Youth STEM Outreach

We partner with local schools and scout troops to introduce the next generation to aerospace through rocketry.

🛡️

FAA-Waivered Range

All launches are conducted on a FAA-waivered site with trained Range Safety Officers ensuring a safe environment.

🌐

National Network

As a NAR section, our members enjoy access to NARAM, regional meets, and thousands of fellow rocketeers nationwide.
