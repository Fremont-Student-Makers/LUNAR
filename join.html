Join LUNAR

Membership gives you unlimited access to our range, mentorship from experienced flyers, voting rights at club meetings, and much more.

Membership Options

Choose Your Plan

All memberships run January 1 – December 31. New members joining mid-year pay a pro-rated fee.

🌱

Junior Member

$20/year

For students age 18 and under. Requires a parent or guardian co-signature.

  • ✅ Unlimited range access (with adult supervisor)
  • ✅ NAR junior membership included
  • ✅ Newsletter & mailing list
  • ✅ Free workshops & build nights
MOST POPULAR
🚀

Adult Member

$40/year

Full membership for adults 19 and over. Everything you need to fly with LUNAR.

  • ✅ Unlimited range access
  • ✅ NAR adult membership included
  • ✅ Newsletter & mailing list
  • ✅ Free workshops & build nights
  • ✅ Voting rights at club meetings
  • ✅ Club discount on motors & supplies
👨‍👩‍👧

Family Membership

$65/year

Covers two adults and all children under 18 at the same address. Best value for families!

  • ✅ All adult member benefits (×2)
  • ✅ All junior member benefits (unlimited kids)
  • ✅ One shared NAR family membership
  • ✅ Priority registration for popular events

Not sure yet?

Come to any launch day as a guest for just $10 — no membership required. You can watch, ask questions, and even help fly a club loaner rocket. We're happy to answer any questions on-site.

📬

Stay in the Loop

Not ready to join yet? You can still subscribe to our free mailing list to receive launch announcements and club news. Email info@lunarrocketry.org with "Subscribe" in the subject.

🤝

Volunteer Opportunities

LUNAR is entirely volunteer-run. We always need help with setup/teardown, Range Safety Officer training, photography, outreach, and web development. Let us know in your application!
